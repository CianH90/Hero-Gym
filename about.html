What we DO

Here at hero gym we welcome a wide range of clients from avid gym goers to people new to the health and fitness scene,
we provide a wide range of services from in-house personal trainers to tailor made workout and nutrition plans,
our goal is to make sure our clients get the most out of their visit.


Services we provide!

personal trainers

Thinking of hiring a personal trainer? We work in conjunction with a number of personal trainers who have a minimum of fiver years experiance in the industry. Come talk to us and we will match you with a trainer to suit your need whether it is an in-house trainer or a trainer to make out-calls we have you covered.

Classes

Come and check out our wide range of classes, from HITT to yoga. Our classes range in fitness level from beginner to advanced gym goers. All gym members get the oppertunity to try each class for free so they can decide if it is the class for them.

Meal Plans

Have you been trying to lose weight to no avail? If so we will tailor a meal plan for you, we will include your favourite foods to make sure you are still enjoying the things you like while living a healtier lifestyle.
